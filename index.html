Balu believes in education to be the ultimate liberation for every human. No child will be left behind. We at GCSE NEWTON focus on different learning capabilities of children. Every child is different in their intellectual capacities. And that's natural and completely alright. Which is a strength rather than a weakness. Don't give a second thought if we could help you or otherwise. Visit us today. No need to take any appointment. Just walk-in, and we are ready to talk to you.

Mind is beautiful, and we at GCSE NEWTON believe in the capability of that mind to conquer fear, anxiety and doubt towards learning something new. We have created this platform to unlock the intellectual beauty in every child. We are instilling confidence in every child. We handhold while they walk towards success. Within a week of teaching, you will observe a substantial difference in your child's energy, enthusiasm, and seriousness towards studies. They may also teach you back what they learn from us. And we encourage that attitude, as learning is enhanced through knowledge-sharing.

Our Services

Subject Training

We will not just follow the curriculum but also interact with children in real-time to discuss the subject and learn back from them. They will be provided with resources at hand to test their knowledge and see where they stand. If there is room for improvement, we start working again. We will work until we ace the GCSEs.

Subject Retention

Some knowledge can be re-learned. But some knowledge must be retained. We call this as baseline foundation upon which the subsequent knowledge is built. Every child will structure their knowledge-reception in such a way that they remember it for a lifetime. Thus, we are proud of ourselves to engage every child with a lifetime experience.

Writing Practice

Retained knowledge should be put on paper. And GCSEs are entirely based on what is written rather than what is known. Every child will be assisted with comprehension and writing. We don't know if they could be authors in future but this will be an excellent starting point.

Fitness and Sports

Health body breeds healthy mind. Every enrolled student will be provided with a subscription to sports centre and Gym which is a minute walk from the centre. Trainers and caretakers at the sports centre will assist them with activities while speaking to them for any special needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you cater to?

We primarily cater to students preparing for their GCSEs, typically ages 14-16. However, we also offer programs for younger students who want to get a head start on their GCSE preparation.

How much does it cost to enroll?

Our fees vary depending on the number of subjects and the level of support required. We offer flexible payment plans to accommodate different budgets. Please contact us for a personalized quote.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online classes to accommodate different learning preferences and situations. Our online platform is interactive and designed to provide the same quality of education as our in-person classes.

How many students are in each class?

We maintain small class sizes, typically 8-12 students per class, to ensure that each student receives adequate attention and support. We also offer one-on-one tutoring for students who need more personalized assistance.

How do I enroll my child?

Enrollment is easy! You can fill out the enrollment form on our website, call us, or visit our center in person. We'll schedule an initial assessment to determine your child's current level and needs, and then recommend the most suitable program.
