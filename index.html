Our Approach

Subject Training

We will not just follow the curriculum but also interact with children in real-time to discuss the subject and learn back from them. They will be provided with resources at hand to test their knowledge and see where they stand. If there is room for improvement, we start working again. We will work until we ace the GCSEs.

Subject Retention

Some knowledge can be re-learned. But some knowledge must be retained. We call this as baseline foundation upon which the subsequent knowledge is built. Every child will structure their knowledge-reception in such a way that they remember it for a lifetime. Thus, we are proud of ourselves to engage every child with a lifetime experience.

Writing Practice

Retained knowledge should be put on paper. And GCSEs are entirely based on what is written rather than what is known. Every child will be assisted with comprehension and writing. We don't know if they could be authors in future but this will be an excellent starting point.

Fitness and Sports

Health body breeds healthy mind. Every enrolled student will be provided with a subscription to sports centre and Gym which is a minute walk from the centre. Trainers and caretakers at the sports centre will assist them with activities while speaking to them for any special needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and approach.

Our classes are structured to provide a balance of theory and practical application. We begin with core concepts, followed by interactive discussions, problem-solving sessions, and regular assessments to track progress. Each student receives personalized attention to address their specific learning needs.

We provide additional support for students who are struggling with specific subjects. This includes one-on-one sessions, extra practice materials, and alternative teaching methods tailored to your child's learning style. Our goal is to ensure every student succeeds, regardless of their starting point.

We use a comprehensive assessment system that includes regular tests, homework evaluation, and practical assignments. Parents receive detailed monthly progress reports and have access to our online portal where they can track their child's performance in real-time. We also schedule quarterly parent-teacher meetings to discuss progress and address any concerns.

GCSE NEWTON stands out for our holistic approach to education. We don't just focus on academic excellence but also on developing critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a genuine love for learning. Our integrated fitness program, personalized learning plans, and emphasis on knowledge retention rather than rote memorization set us apart from traditional tutoring centers.
